Duplicate Encircle Claim Step 1 — Test first

Creates a full copy of any Encircle claim — same metadata, all rooms duplicated. Use this to safely test migration on the copy before touching the real claim.

Migrate Old Rooms Step 2 — Move photos & delete old rooms

Detects rooms containing DAY0 / DAY # 0 (e.g. 8101 TRAVEL .0 …. FOYER ... DAY0 MC READINGS ..), moves any photos to the matching new-format room, then deletes the old room. Run on the duplicate first to verify, then on the real claim.
